Donate to nirsoft.net With PayPal

NirSoft Web site provides more than 100 freeware utilities for Windows without requiring the users to pay any fee, even when other companies sell similar tools for dozens of $. However, maintaining this Web site, updating the existing utilities and releasing new utilities require me to invest a fair amount of time, and also money (especially for hosting).

If you think that the utilities of NirSoft are useful and you want to say thanks for making them available for free, and support the development of future versions, you're welcomed to donate some money to NirSoft by using the PayPal Donate form below. You can choose to send any amount you like. After filling the currency and the amount values, click the 'Continue' button, and you'll redirected to PayPal Web site to complete the donation process.

Notice: If you don't have a PayPal account and you don't want to create one, you can still donate by clicking the "Continue" link under the "Don't have a PayPal account?" section.
